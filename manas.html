The Indian Culture

We all know that India is a vast country. It has an area of 3.287 million square kilometers. It ranks seventh after Russia, Cannada, China, USA, Brazil and Austrailia. In India people of all religions live. In India there are 28 states and 9 union teritories. The population of India is 1.38 billion. Over the centuries, there has been a significant fusion of cultures between Buddhists, Hindus, Muslims, Jains, Sikhs and various tribal populations in India. India is the birthplace of Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, Sikhism, and other religons. They are collectively known as Indian religons.
